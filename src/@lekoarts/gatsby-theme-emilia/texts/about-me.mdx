## About Me

Naoto Nishida is a master's student in the Graduate School of Interdisciplinary Information Studies, at [University of Tokyo](https://www.iii.u-tokyo.ac.jp/).  
His research interests lie in Human-Computer Interaction, especially in adapting human behaviors and cognitions to the learning process in order to enhance the efficiency of learning.  

東京大学大学院学際情報学府の修士1年生です．  
言語学習が好きです．人間の認知や行動の特徴を学習の支援に応用することに興味があります．
