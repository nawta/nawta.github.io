## About Me

Naoto Nishida is a Bachelor's student in the College of Media Arts, Science and Technology in the School of Informatics, at [University of Tsukuba](https://www.tsukuba.ac.jp/).  
His research interests lie in Human-Computer Interaction, especially in adapting human behaviors and cognitions to the learning process in order to enhance the efficiency of learning.  

筑波大学情報学群情報メディア創成学類の学部4年生です．  
言語学習が好きなので，人間の認知や行動の特徴を学習の支援に応用することに興味があります．
