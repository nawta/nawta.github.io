## About Me

(En)
Naoto Nishida is a 1st year PhD candidate in the Graduate School of Interdisciplinary Information Studies, at [University of Tokyo](https://www.iii.u-tokyo.ac.jp/), working with [Yoshio Ishiguro](https://lab.rekimoto.org/members/yoshio-ishiguro/) and [Jun Rekimoto](https://lab.rekimoto.org/members/rekimoto/).
Previously, I was working with Prof. [Jun Rekimoto](https://lab.rekimoto.org/members/rekimoto/) in my master's, with Prof. [Buntarou Shizuki](https://www.iplab.cs.tsukuba.ac.jp/~shizuki/index-e.html) in my bachelor's, with Prof. [Xiang Anthony Chen](https://hci.prof/) while I was a visiting scholar at [UCLA](https://www.ucla.edu/), and Dr. [Kaori Ikematsu](https://sites.google.com/view/ikema2) while my internship at Yahoo!JAPAN. 
His research interests lie widely in Human-Computer Interaction, Human-AI Interaction, Natural Language Processing and Machine Learning, especially ones relevant to language, learning, or communication(e.g., AI-based learning, AI-assisted communication, etc).
